Company Registration in India – An Overview

Setting a Private Limited Company is one of the highly recommended ways to start a business in India. This type of company offers limited liability for its shareholders with certain restrictions placed on the ownership. An LLP has partners, who own and manage the business. Whereas in private limited company registration, directors may be different from shareholders. BNG Filings, your leading legal consultant, offers quick Company Registration service in India at nominal pricing. Here you will find how you can register your company.

The Benefits

– Shields from personal liability and protects from other risks and losses;

– Attracts more customers;

– Offers liability protection to protect your company’s assets;

– Greater capital contribution and greater stability;

– Increases the potential to grow big and expand by being favourable to investors.

Requirements

A. Having atleast two directors:

B. A unique name;

C. Minimum authorised capital of Rs. 1 lakh;

D. A registered office, be it commercial or residential.

Steps Involved

1. Application for DSC (Digital Signature Certificate).

2. Apply for the DIN (Director Identification Number)

3. Application for the name availability.

4. Filing of the EMoa and eAoA to register a private limited company.

5. Apply for the PAN and TAN of the company.

6. Issued certificate of incorporation by RoC with PAN and TAN.

7. Opening a current bank account on the company name.

Documents Required

– Identity and address proof all the directors and the shareholders

– Registered office proof

Why BNG Filings

The Private Limited Company Registration process is completely online, so you don’t even have to leave your home to get your entity registered. At Vakilsearch, we complete the Company Registration online within 14 days.
